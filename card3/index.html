Singdi Village

Discover the charm of Singdi Village, nestled in the picturesque Lamjung district of Nepal. Home to over 300 houses, this vibrant community thrives on farming and animal husbandry. The villagers take great pride in their cultural heritage, worshipping their own deity, Lauga Devi.
